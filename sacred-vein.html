Book One

The Sacred Vein

Five generations. One obsession. A secret that has killed everyone who came close to finding it.

Historian Ammon Lundquist grew up in the shadow of his family's obsession: a legendary treasure hidden somewhere in Utah's Uinta Mountains — Carre-Shinob, the Lost Rhoades Mine — that claimed his father's life and consumed generations before him. Now the search falls to him, and the mountains are still keeping score.

Book Two

The Shadow Archive

The chamber was just the beginning.

Six months after uncovering an impossible secret beneath Utah's Uinta Mountains, Professor Ammon Lundquist thought the danger was behind him. He was wrong. A hidden message in his ancestor's locket reveals that Friedrich Hathenbruck — the German physician who first discovered the sacred chamber — kept secrets even from his own journals.

Book Three

The Hidden Network

The saga's third installment spans continents and centuries.

Eighteen months on, the hunt goes global. Dual expeditions, cave collapses, hostage standoffs, and stakes that stay stubbornly, thrillingly human. The Sacred Vein saga reaches its widest scope yet.
